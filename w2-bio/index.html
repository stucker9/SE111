Bio

I am 32 years old. I am working at Operation Stand Down Rhode Island in the legal department as the Intake Claims Coordinator for VA Disability. I carry a few other hats as well, IT certainly being one of them. I enjoy helping my coworkers simplify their processes to enable them more time to dedicate to assisting veterans. I also enjoy helping veterans and have been working the front desk for a little while now so I have a big picture of how this non-profit works. I am proud of my job because I am also a veteran myself so I feel right at home there helping others.
